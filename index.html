about us:

“Nami” (波) means “wave” in Japanese — it evokes calmness, rhythm, and flow.
Combined with “Matcha”, it suggests the wave of calm energy that comes from drinking matcha.
Our company is aimed to reflect movement, nature, and mindfulness, all core ideas in matcha culture.
